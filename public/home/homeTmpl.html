About This Page

This is the beginning of a project to continue honing skills around AngularJS and using services like Firebase. New features and functionality will be added as time goes on. Until then, please feel free to search for a brewery by name and if so inclined, register on the Login page and beginning saving your favorites to your profile. You can also find additional information by clicking on eage breweries name, including details of when they were established, links to their websites, and a description if available.
